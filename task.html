Chaos On Emirates Flight As Indians Rush To Disembark After Trump Approves $100K H-1B Visa Fee

A panic wave hit Indian travellers on Saturday when US President Donald Trump signed an executive order that levied an incredible USD 100,000 charge on fresh H-1B visa petitions.The sudden action generated cofusion among H-1B visa applicants and holders, many of whom were planning to travel between India and United States.

The fallout was the hardest felt on an Emirates flight to India, where panic erupted just seconds before takeoff. According to video streamed by passengers on social media,several Indians chose to leave the plane, fearing they would be banned from returning to the US under the new policy.

In one of the videos, passengers had been standing in the aisles, some furiously scrolling throught their phones , others gazing uncertainly around them. The captain, speaking to the cabin , recogonised the "unprecedented circumstances" and offered passengers an option to get off.

"Ladies and gentlemen, this is your captain speaking. In light of the current extraordinary situation, we realise some passengers no longer desire to proceed with their flight. Tht's perfectly okay, but if you'd like to disembark, now is your chance." he stated.
